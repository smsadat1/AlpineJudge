make one single end function -> SpawnContainer()
It'll recieve args -> Execrules, Client & Context
These are also the inputs of the container pipeline itself.
Pipeline will produce -> result.json, stdout.log, stderr.log  
Then upload on S3


Container pipeline: 

Take Execrules          [call SpawnContainer()]
    ↓
Pull Container Image    [call pullContainerImage()]
    ↓
Prepare OCI specs       [call enforceContainerLimits()]
    ↓
Run Container           [call startContainer()]
    ↓
Produce result.json stdout.log stderr.log [startContainer() creates these artifacts]
    ↓
Upload to S3            [startContainer() uploads artifacts]
    ↓
Destroy container   [startContainer() destroys container during exit]
